La Mélancolie
Louis-Jean-François Lagrenée

La Mélancolie is one of the most emotional works of art from the 18th century which still carries the theme of melancholy and sadness in art. Created in 1785 by Louis-Jean-François Lagrenée, La Mélancolie is a popular sad painting that represents the trending style, Rococo. La Mélancolie is a simple yet impactful demonstration of its title and depicts a young woman dressed in soft, flowing clothing with her head resting on an arm supported by her thighs. This is a classic representation of depression that is familiar to everyone who experiences it. The woman's body language means a lot and is evident by the dark backdrop of the painting and the glare of her laser eyes as she stares into what is assumed to be mental oblivion.
