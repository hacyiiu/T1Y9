Operation True Love

High school student Su-ae Shim suddenly gets something mysterious which contains an old cell phone. Apparently, the old cell phone contained a lot of information about Su-ae and lots of photos of beautiful girls. Interesting also because the phone can track the amount of love that can be received in his life. Unfortunately, the amount of love Su-ae received was 0. Then came a mysterious person who gave her a cell phone and a chance to increase the amount of love she has but there is a time limit. If Su-ae fails she will be eliminated. Well at first, he ignores it because he has a girlfriend. But then his girlfriend cheated on him with his foster sister named Ra-im. Ra-im is a beautiful, smart and popular student. The two of them used to be close, but ever since Su-ae saw Ra-im, she was furious. Currently Su-ae has two missions, namely to increase the amount of love and the second is to get revenge on her boyfriend.
