Suzume

Suzume, a 17-year-old girl, lives in southwest Japan, in a quiet town called Kyushu had a dream that her mother gave her a broken chair in a magical world. One day, she met a young man named Souta who told her about abandoned places in the area and was looking for the door. Suzume then followed in Souta's footsteps. She found an old door that is tall in the middle of the water, as if it is protected from disaster that happened and destroyed its surroundings. Suzume was mesmerized by the door so she is trying reach for the knob and her body was pulled inside the door. The door begin to open one by one across Japan and cause destruction to anyone who gets near them. Souta is the one who is supposed to do the mission of closing the open door, but failed because he transformed into a chair. This made Suzume have to close all the door across Japan to prevent disaster.
