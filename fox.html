The Fox Club

A student named Park Onyu in grade 3 of junior high school has no friends to talk to. There is a new student named Shin Chaeri who enters Onyu's school. They communicate and become best friends and transfer to high school together. Chaeri is very beautiful, she is offered to enter a special club for beautiful people. But Chaeri didn't want to because Onyu wasn't invited. The fox club member allows Onyu to also join the club. Back then, the fox club was very peaceful and always together, but after Chaeri and Onyu entered, everything changed. The club captain is Kim Ilmi, has a bestfriend named Han Huiseung since junior high school. Huiseung was interested in Chaeri, they became close because they often talked and finally dating. Ilmi disliked and took revenge on both of them by approaching them and betray them. Ilmi was a trainee at a management company because she wanted to become an idol. She always fail to debut because of his lack of singing and dancing skills. The manager in management saw that Ilmi was close to Chaeri, so she asked Chaeri to join as a trainee. Chaeri signed a contract to become an idol and joined the Ilmi group. They gathered in a meeting room, the manager announced that they will debut. Ilmu was shocked because she had been practicing since she was little while Chaeri had not done anything but is able to debut. But in the end, they debut together.
