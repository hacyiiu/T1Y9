See You Again
Tyler, The Creator

The song "See You Again" by Tyler, The Creator and Kali Uchis is about the desire and nostalgia for someone's presence; a kind of love story. The song is about how the narrator's eyes are always in search of their love that lives in their dreams and only emerges in their minds when they take refuge in their sleep. Although the desire for this person is strong, the narrator knows that it is not entirely real and is willing to take as much time as possible before reality takes over and the time has come to separate. Ultimately, the song encourages listeners not to waste time and make the most of the moments they can spend with the people they care about.
