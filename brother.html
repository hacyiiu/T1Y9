My Three Annoying Brother

A girl named Haeju Kim didn't have any friends when she was 7, because she was often bullied. She has 3 older siblings, Hanyong, Duyong, Saeyong who cares but doesn’t look like they care. Her three older brothers are very famous because of their good looks, and many girls want to be with them. Haeju had warned them to pretend they didn't know her at school, she didn't want to meet her older brothers at school. Haeju wanted to take revenge on her brother for disobeying her warning, but failed. Haeju likes a guy named Won Do who not handsome. But the one who likes Haeju is Won Do who is handsome. Haeju confesses on Valentine's Day to the unattractive Won Do, but the attractive one accepts the letter and they had a miscommunication. Haeju joins the comic club because she has a crush there. Haeju attends the comic club, so handsome Won Do also joins the comic club. Won Do has better drawing skills than Haeju since their first meeting. Haeju asked to be taught at home. While at his house, Won Do is judged by his overprotective older brother and finally get along after many small dramas.
