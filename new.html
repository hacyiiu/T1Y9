New Person, Same Old Mistakes
Tame Impala

The song "New Person, Same Old Mistakes" by Tame Impala is about confronting the fear of making the same mistakes again when embarking on a new venture. The singer recognizes that one must courageously take risks to achieve something new, even if there is a chance it will fail or lead to regret. The song expresses the idea that, rather than be held back by fear of the same negative outcomes, one's mistakes should instead help inform better decisions moving forward. This is illustrated in the bridge which reminds us that our experiences, though difficult, can help us learn, grow, and make better choices in the future.
