After Hours

This song is about carrying on the relationships and love even during hard times. The Weeknd sings about how fighting, loneliness, and insecurities often divide him and his loved one. Despite his mistakes, he promises to treat his loved one better and to always be there for them. He knows that his love for them is strong enough to withstand the struggles and his mistakes, as long as his loved one gives him another chance. The song is ultimately about finding lasting love despite the trying times, and it emphasizes the importance of being understanding and trusting with one's partner.
